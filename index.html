Introducing the ultimate risk free way

for jobless veterans to gain a quality education in computer science and build a career for themselves in the software world

The Problem

Veterans lack transferrable skills from the military to transition back into society.

The Solution

A coding bootcamp and laptop with the guarantee of a job placement and connections at the end.

What Do Veterans Gain?

Veterans gain a skill set that pays and prevents them from becoming homeless again and thus building them a sustainable career.

How Do I Pay For This?

The camp and laptop are completely free upfront! We will take 15% from your first year salary in order to sustain our program to help more veterans.
